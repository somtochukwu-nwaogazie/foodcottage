Food cottage was established in 2013 after years of catering for so many events, social and coperate events successfully.

Food cottage is not a fast food but we prepare fresh food fast. We pride ourselves with excellence and precision and we are always happy to satisfy our customers.

Food cottage is located at 4b Elekhaia Estate. We specialize in continental and intercontinental cuisines at very affordable prices. We cater for all kinds of events at prices that can suit everyone who likes good food. With us, time is of the essence

At food cottage, we serve all kinds of African dishes. We are specially known for our traditional dishes like Banga soup, Rivers native soup, Afang soup, Ofe Onugbo, Ofe nsala/ White soup, Edikaikong, Oha soup, Owo soup, Efo riro, Okro soup, White rice and different sauces, pepper jollof rice, moin-moin, nice beans & plantain, Unripe plantain porridge,etc.

We also specialize in preparing different pots of soups for mothers and wives with very busy schedule who sometimes don't have time for home cooking, and also bachelors who have missed mama's cooking. We give them home away from home cooking. We have excellent chefs who make good food possible at very affordable prices.

We are open Mondays - Sundays. We deliver food to any location, our courier services is always available and we are happy to serve.
